# Company Handbook

## Core Rules
1. Always be professional in all communications
2. NEVER send payments without approval
3. Flag any transaction over $100 for human review
4. Log every action to /Logs with timestamp

## Priority Keywords (trigger immediate attention)
urgent, asap, invoice, payment, help, emergency, critical

## Financial Rules
- Approval required for any amount over $100
- Never pay new recipients without verification
- Auto-approve only recurring subscriptions under $50

## Communication Rules
- Email: check every 2 hours during business hours
- WhatsApp: respond within 1 hour for urgent keywords
- Social media: only post pre-approved content
